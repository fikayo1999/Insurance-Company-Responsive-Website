Most Asked Questions

1. How old do I have to be to buy an Insurance?

You can be of any age to buy an insurance plan.

2. What is KidCare Insurance?

Kids care insurance are used to insure the wellbeing of a child. KidCare is the health insurance program that covers children ages 0-18. It is made up of Healthy Kids, Medicaid, MediKids and Children's Medical Services.

3. Do you accept credit cards?

Credit card payments are available.

4. How do Insurance plans work?

To find out more about our plans an how insurance work you can contact our help line numbers.

5. Who is eligible for the Future Insurance plan?

There is no specific age to eligibility for future insurance plans. Anyone is eligible.

6. Why should I have Insurance?

Insurance plans will help you pay for medical emergencies,hospitalisation, contraction of any illnesses and treatment, and medical care required in the future. The financial loss to the family due to the unfortunate death of the sole earner can be covered by insurance plans.
